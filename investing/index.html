Getting into the details

How pricing works
Our Digital plan, which is a fit for most customers, has an annual fee of 0.25% per year on your balance—or about $25 per year for every $10,000 invested. There's no minimum balance on your account, ever. For more in-depth advice on higher balances, we offer a Premium plan for an annual fee of 0.40%. Select the plan that works for you.
How our investments work
We manage your money and invest it in a recommended portfolio when you deposit. We handle the trading and rebalancing for you.
Help with your account or finances
Our customer support team is available five days a week to answer questions about your account. If you want in-depth financial advice, you can talk to a certified advisor by phone. Our Premium plan provides you with unlimited access and costs 0.40% per year on your invested balances. We also offer advice packages for needs of all kinds, starting at $199.
Satisfaction guaranteed
We're committed to providing you with a great financial experience. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll do everything we can to make it right.
