Checking and Finance Reserve

There are no fees on your balance in either Finance Reserve or Checking products, so you can try either product out without worry. Instead of charging you fees, LOFVAL receives payments from its program banks for Finance Reserve, and LOFVAL Financial LLC earns revenue from merchant interchange fees and net interest margin on balances in Checking accounts.

Getting into the details

Pricing for LOFVAL

Explore our pricing plans in full. Here are the basics.

  • Checking has no maintenance fees, no overdraft fees, no minimum balance, and ATM fees are reimbursed.
  • Finance Reserve goals don't incur fees from the account balance. Learn more about how we earn money on Finance.
  • Investing and GOOD CHANCESment accounts have a fee of 0.25% per year on your balance—about $25 per year for every $10,000 invested.

We don't require a minimum balance. The minimum deposit is $10.

Help with your account or finances

Customer support team members are available five days per week to answer questions about your account.

If you want in-depth financial advice, you can talk to a certified advisor by phone. Our Premium plan provides you with unlimited access and costs 0.40% per year on your invested balances. We also offer advice packages for needs of all kinds, starting at $199.

Account security and privacy

We take the security of our customers' accounts seriously. We employ strong browser encryption, secure data storage, and more. For details, read about our security practices.

We respect and protect the privacy of your information and will never share your data with a third party without your permission. Learn more about our Privacy Policy.
